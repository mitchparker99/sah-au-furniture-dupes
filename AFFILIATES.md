# Affiliate application prep — au-furniture-dupes

Everything you need on hand when applying. Both networks approve
content/comparison sites; the live site is the main asset they review.

## What to say (both networks)

- Site: https://mitchparker99.github.io/sah-au-furniture-dupes/
  (swap to lookalikeliving.com.au once the domain is bought and pointed)
- Property type: content / price-comparison site
- Audience: Australian furniture shoppers researching designer pieces and
  budget alternatives
- Promotional method: editorial product comparisons with similarity scoring;
  no paid-search brand bidding, no coupon/loyalty, no incentivised clicks
- Traffic: new site, organic SEO + Pinterest/Instagram content planned
- Disclosure: affiliate disclosure on every page footer + methodology page

## 1. Commission Factory (commissionfactory.com)

AU-focused network. Covers: Temple & Webster, Freedom, Mocka, Interior
Secrets, Luxo Living, Adairs and most AU home retailers in the catalogue.

1. Sign up as an Affiliate (free) with the details above.
2. Once the account is approved, apply to each advertiser program:
   Temple & Webster first (most catalogue links), then Interior Secrets,
   Luxo Living, Mocka, Freedom, Adairs.
3. Put the publisher/click ID into the repo secret `AFFILIATE_CF_ID`
   (repo: mitchparker99/sah-au-furniture-dupes -> Settings -> Secrets).
   Links upgrade automatically on the next nightly build.

## 2. Impact (impact.com)

Covers: Castlery AU, West Elm. Same story as above; apply to those two
brand programs after the marketplace account is approved. ID goes into
`AFFILIATE_IMPACT_ID`.

## Notes

- Kmart, IKEA and Matt Blatt have no mainstream AU affiliate program right
  now - their links stay clean UTM outbound (fine; they build trust).
- Both networks may ask for a privacy policy page - ROADMAP item; say yes
  and we ship one before first payout.
- Never bid on retailer brand terms in paid search; it is the most common
  reason comparison sites get ejected from programs.
